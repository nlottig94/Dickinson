Dickinson, Emily, 1830-1886. Poems: Packet XXVII, Fascicle 19. Includes 13 poems, written in ink, ca. 1862. Houghton Library, Harvard University, Cambridge, Mass.
Houghton Library - (147) Knows how to forget! J433, Fr391
Publication History
The version in Fascicle 19 (A) was published in BM (1945), 305, as four quatrains, among the poems incomplete or unfinished, from a transcript of A (a tr757b). The version in Set 7 (B) was published in BM (1945), 116-17. Poems (1955), 335-37 (A principal, B principal); CP (1960), 207-8 (B). MB (1981), 425 (A) and 1220-21 (B), in facsimile. (J433). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F391A).
