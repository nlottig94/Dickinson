Publication History
Poems (1896), 166, stanzas 5 and 6. Stanzas 1-3 and the first two lines of stanza 4 were published in BM (1945), 100-101, from a transcript of A (a tr499); the revisions for lines 4 and 11 were adopted. Poems (1955), 450-51; CP (1960), 287-88, with all of the revisions adopted.  MB (1981), 429-30, in facsimile. (J588). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F394A).
