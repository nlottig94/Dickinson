Poem 6 (J 75: 1830-1886)

1:
She died at play,
She died at play,
She died at play,
She died at play,
2:
Gambolled away
Gambolled away
Gambolled away
Gambolled away
3:
Her lease of spotted hours,
Her lease of spotted hours,
Her lease of spotted hours,
Her lease of spotted hours,
4:
Then sank as gaily as a Turk
Then sank as gaily as a Turk
Then sank as gaily as a Turk
Then sank as gaily as a Turk
5:
Upon a Couch of flowers.
Upon a Couch of flowers.
Upon a couch of flowers.
Upon a Couch of flowers.
6:
Her ghost strolled softly o'er the hill
Her ghost strolled softly o'er the hill
Her ghost strolled softly o'er the hill
Her ghost strolled softly o'er the hill
7:
Yesterday , and Today ,
Yesterday , and Today ,
Yesterday and today ,
Yesterday , and Today ,
8:
Her vestments as the silver fleece —
Her vestments as the silver fleece —
Her vestments as the silver fleece ,
Her vestments as the silver fleece —
9:
Her countenance as spray.
Her countenance as spray.
Her countenance as spray.
Her countenance as spray.
