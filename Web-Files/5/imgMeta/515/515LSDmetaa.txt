Publication History
Poems (1891), 136-37, from the fascicle (B), with the second stanza omitted and the remainder as seven quatrains. Bingham, AB (1945), 39, the second stanza, from the fascicle (B); Bingham, New England Quarterly, 20 (March 1947), 11-12 (B), corrected and entire. Poems (1955), 55-57 (A principal, B summarized); CP (1960), 36-37 (A). MB (1981), 83-84 (B), in facsimile.  (J70). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F117A).
