Amherst - Amherst Manuscript #fascicle 80 - There is a word - asc:12099 - p. 12

Publication History
Poems (1896), 19, with both stanzas.  Poems (1955), 42-43, the first stanza only, from the holograph fragment then available, though noting that a second stanza had appeared in 1896; CP (1960), 30, the first stanza only. The missing manuscript was reported in Franklin, American Literature, 50 (March 1978), 114-15. MB (1981), 30, in facsimile. (J57). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F55A).
