Analysis of Emily Dickinson's Fascicles

As mentioned on our About page, our team has focused mainly analyzed the variations within Emily Dickinson's own poems and the variations between the original manuscripts and the many published versions. We have done this in two ways, looking at the reduction of dashes in the published versions compared to the original versions and looking at how many variants are shared among all versions of the poems.

Please visit the SVG graphs that we have produced for Fascicle 6 and Fascicle 16 that analyze the reduction of dashes and, also, the Cytoscape network analyses for Fascicle 6 and Fascicle 16 that look at the sharing of variants among all versions of the poems.

You can also navigate to these analyses using the buttons on the left side of the page.
