Publication History
BM (1945), 324, from the copy to Bowles (A), arranged like the fascicle copy (B), except for the last stanza, which consists of three lines; a transcript of B (a 713c) was available to the editor. Poems (1955), 134-35 (A principal, B); CP (1960), 88 (A). MB (1981), 151-52 (B), in facsimile. (J186). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F237B).
