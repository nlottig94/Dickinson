Poem 3 (J 279: 1861/1896)

1:
Tie the Strings to my Life , My Lord,
Tie the strings to my life , my Lord,
Tie the strings to my life , my Lord,
Tie the Strings to my Life , My Lord,
2:
Then , I am ready to go!
Then I am ready to go!
Then I am ready to go!
Then , I am ready to go!
3:
Just a look at the Horses —
Just a look at the horses —
Just a look at the horses —
Just a look at the Horses —
4:
Rapid! That will do!
5:
Put me in on the tightest highest—firmest side —
Put me in on the firmest side ,
Put me on the firmest side ,
Put me in on the firmest side —
6:
So I shall never fall —
So I shall never fall ;
So I shall never fall ;
So I shall never fall —
7:
For we must ride to the Judgment —
For we must ride to the Judgment ,
For we must ride to the Judgment ,
For we must ride to the Judgment —
8:
And it's partly, down Hill—many a mile—the Hills
And it's partly down hill.
And it's partly down hill.
And it's partly, down Hill—
9:
But never I mind the steepest—Bridges
But never I mind the bridges,
But never I mind the bridges,
But never I mind the steepest—
10:
And never I mind the Sea —
And never I mind the sea ;
And never I mind the sea ;
And never I mind the Sea —
11:
Held fast in Everlasting Race —
Held fast in everlasting race
Held fast in everlasting race
Held fast in Everlasting Race —
12:
By my own Choice , and Thee —
By my own choice and thee .
By my own choice and thee .
By my own Choice , and Thee —
13:
Good bye to the Life I used to live —
Good-by to the life I used to live ,
Good-by to the life I used to live ,
Goodbye to the Life I used to live —
14:
And the World I used to know —
And the world I used to know ;
And the world I used to know ;
And the World I used to know —
15:
And kiss the Hills , for me, just once —
And kiss the hills for me, just once ;
And kiss the hills for me, just once ;
And kiss the Hills , for me, just once —
16:
Here's a keepsake for Then—Now I am ready to go!
Now I am ready to go!
Now I am ready to go!
Then— I am ready to go!
