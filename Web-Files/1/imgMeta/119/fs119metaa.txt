Amherst Manuscript #fascicle 82
Amherst - Amherst Manuscript #fascicle 82 - The gentian weaves her fringes - asc:15602 - p. 6

Publication History
Springfield Daily Republican (18 May 1886), 4, the first four lines from Susan's copy (B), concluding her obituary of ed; reprinted in LL (1924), 105. Poems (1891), 186, entire, from the fascicle (C), made composite with readings from lines 6 and 10 of the Norcross transcript ([A]), which Mabel Todd received during her preparation of the volume. Poems (1955), 26 (C only), with the conjecture that the text in Poems (1891) may have derived from a different copy; CP (1960), 19 (C). Higgins, American Literature, 38 (March 1966), 19, from the Norcross transcript ([A]). The copy to Susan (B) appeared in Paul C. Richards, Catalogue 82 (1978), 21, entire, with facsimile, and Christie's, Manuscripts and Printed Books (15 December 1995), 19. MB (1981), 13 (C), in facsimile. (J27). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F18C).
BM (1945), 195, as two quatrains.  Poems (1955), 27, as two quatrains; also CP (1960), 19. MB (1981), 13-14, in facsimile. (J28). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F19A).
 -History from Franklin Variorum 1998
