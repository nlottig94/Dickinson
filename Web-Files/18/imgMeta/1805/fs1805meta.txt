Dickinson, Emily, 1830-1886.  Poems: Packet VI, Fascicle 18. Includes 13 poems, written in ink, dated ca. 1862.
Houghton Library - (24b) He strained my faith -, J497, Fr366
Publication History
BM (1945), 304, from a transcript of A (a tr495), as two quatrains, among the poems incomplete or unfinished. The alternatives were adopted; a note explained that "In Mrs. Todd's copy the last two words are inverted." Poems (1955), 380-81; CP (1960), 240. MB (1981), 386, in facsimile. (J497). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F366A).
