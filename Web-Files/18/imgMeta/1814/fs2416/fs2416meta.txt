Houghton Library - (70d) My Reward for Being - was This, J343, Fr375
Publication History
Both versions are presented in BM (1945), 308-9, among the poems incomplete or unfinished, from transcripts of A and B (a tr518 and 518a). The first is arranged as three quatrains; for the second, a footnote provided the text of the variant last stanza. Poems (1955), 274-75 (A principal, B); CP (1960), 163 (A). MB (1981), 400 (A) and 550 (B), in facsimile. (J343). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F375B).
