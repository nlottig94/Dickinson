Publication History
CP (1924), 313, from Susan's copy (B), in 10 lines. It was included among the poems published in SH (1914), though it had not appeared there. Poems (1955), 230-31 (A, B principal); CP (1960), 145-46 (B). MB (1981), 304 (A), in facsimile. (J310). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F422B).
