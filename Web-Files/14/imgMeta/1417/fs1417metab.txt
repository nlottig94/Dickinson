Houghton Library - (49a, b) It ceased to hurt me, though so slow, J584, Fr421; Give little Anguish, J310, Fr422
Publication History
Atlantic Monthly, 143 (February 1929), 186, and FP (1929), 189, with stanza 3 omitted and the rest as three stanzas of 6, 4, and 4 lines; in later collections, as three quatrains. The entire poem, arranged as three quatrains and a final couplet, was published in Bingham, New England Quarterly, 20 (March 1947), 36, from a transcript of A. In none of these appearances were the alternatives adopted. Poems (1955), 446-47; CP (1960), 285, with all of the alternatives adopted. MB (1981), 303-4, in facsimile. (J584). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F421A).
CP (1924), 313, from Susan's copy (B), in 10 lines. It was included among the poems published in SH (1914), though it had not appeared there. Poems (1955), 230-31 (A, B principal); CP (1960), 145-46 (B). MB (1981), 304 (A), in facsimile. (J310). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F422A).
