Houghton Library - (112a) One Year ago - jots what? J296, Fr301
Publication History
BM (1945), 159-60, as seven stanzas of 5, 6, 5, 4, 4, 7, and 6 lines. The text derived from a Todd transcript of A (a tr524) in which the alternatives for lines 4 and 11 had been incorporated into lines 5 and 10 respectively and those for lines 8 and 27 had been rejected. Poems (1955), 215-16; CP (1960), 138-39, with the alternative for line 11 adopted.  MB (1981), 241-43, in facsimile. (J296). Franklin Variorum 1998 (F301A).
